                          J U D G M E N T

The case of the petitioner is that he presented Ext.P3 document for registration and according to him the document is liable to be registered as a rectification deed. It is contended that when Ext.P3 was presented for registration before the 3rd respondent, the 3rd respondent returned the document demanding that the stamp duty as payable on sale deed should be remitted. It is with this complaint, this writ petition has been filed.

2. When the writ petition came up for on 23.12.2010, the case was adjourned for the learned Government Pleader to get instructions in the matter. Instructions received by the learned Government Pleader is to the effect that the petitioner has not so far presented Ext.P3 document for registration and that therefore the 3rd respondent did not have any occasion to return the document as alleged by him. Now that such a dispute has been raised in the absence of anything to contradict the same, the only order that can be passed in the writ W.P (C) No. 38361 of 2010 2 petition is that it will be open to the petitioner to present the original of Ext.P3 before the 3rd respondent in which event, the 3rd respondent will consider the request of the petitioner for registering it as a rectification deed and if the document cannot be registered as sought for by the petitioner, appropriate orders will be passed in the matter.

Petitioner shall produce a copy of this judgment along with a copy of the writ petition before the 3rd respondent for compliance.

Writ petition is disposed of as above.
